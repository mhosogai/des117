    THRIFT STORE HOURS
    STORE UPDATES

    THRIFT HOURS OF OPERATION:

    Tuesday thru Saturday 10AM-6:30PM

    WHAT

    Clothing, Household items, Collectibles, Books, Toys, Furniture in good, saleable condition as space permits. Furniture donations must be approved by manager or floor supervisor.

    WHERE

    Bring items in through Thrift Shop to the back of Business Office.

    Leave in designated Donations Area.

    WHEN

    Any time the Thrift Store is open (Tues.-Sat., 10am-6:30pm) as space is available.

    Call store first to find out if space is available (530-759-9648).

    TAX RECEIPT

    May be obtained from Thrift cashier.

    MONETARY DONATIONS
